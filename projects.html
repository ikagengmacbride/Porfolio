Hello, This is my project

This device opens the inner door of the elevator cab and the outer door of the elevator shaft simultaneously at each floor. The controller interacts with the door opening device by sending signals to open or close the doors and by receiving signals when the doors have been completely opened or closed this is done using ladder logic programming on Siemens Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) Simatic S7 1200.
